$_REQUEST is "an associative array consisting of the contents of 
$_GET, $_POST, and $_COOKIE. 

This is a 'superglobal', or automatic global, variable. This simply 
means that it is available in all scopes throughout a script."

        -- authors at www.php.net

For more information please see the official documentation at:
 
http://www.php.net/manual/en/reserved.variables.php#reserved.variables.request
