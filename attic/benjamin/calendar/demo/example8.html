Two Coordinated Dates
  • This demo uses the system theme.
  • Click a button.
  • Notice that you cannot choose days prior to today.
  • You cannot choose a check-in date later then the check-out date.
  • You cannot choose a check-out date prior to the check-in date.
  • Clicking the Reset buttone clears the dates.
  • The default date in the second calendar is the date chosen in the first calendar. This is useful when the check-in date is several months in the future, so the user doesn't need to scroll twice.
