Choose Multiple Days
  • This demo uses the maroon theme.
  • Click the '...' button to display the calendar.
  • You can select multiple days in the calendar.
  • The following dates have been programmatically selected:
    • 2004 April 25
    • 2004 April 18
    • 2004 April 22
    • 2004 April 05
    You can uncheck these.
  • When you are finished, click the 'X' in the top right corner to close the calendar.
  • The selected dates are displayed in the text area.
